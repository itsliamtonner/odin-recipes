Salsa

Description

This quick and easy salsa is so tasty!

Ingredients

  • 6 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • Â¾ cup green chile peppers, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine tomatoes, onion, and chile peppers in a strainer set over a bowl; drain briefly.
  2. Transfer tomato mixture to a clean bowl. Stir in vinegar and salt.
