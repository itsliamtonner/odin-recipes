Tacos

Description

These crispy fish tacos with shredded cabbage and a spicy homemade white sauce are just delicious! Serve with homemade pico de gallo and lime wedges to squeeze on top.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound cod fillets, cut into 2 to 3 ounce portions
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour, or more as needed
  • 1 (12 ounce) package corn tortillas
  • ½ medium head cabbage, finely shredded
  • ¼ cup beer

Steps

  1. Dip floured fish pieces into beer batter. Set aside.
  2. Fry in hot oil until crisp and golden brown. Drain on paper towels. Lightly fry tortillas in hot oil until just crisped, but not too crisp. Drain on paper towels.
  3. Place fried fish in tortillas; top with shredded cabbage and white sauce.
